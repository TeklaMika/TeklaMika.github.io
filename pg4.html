Filters

This website is dedicated to showcasing my favourite hobby: travel photography

The photos are taken by me, Tekla Mikadze. This page touches on the subject of filters

As I mentioned before, my aesthetic is heavily influenced by vintage art, which means that I try to emulate a nostalgia inducing look every time I shoot my photos. The mood of a piece is mostly dictated by its colors, so altering those colors post-production is a great way to change up photos. It's arguably the best part of developing pictures each photo has its own specific set of aspects that need to be edited a certain way, which keeps the editing process exciting and not monotonous or boring. I avoid pre-made filters and really do treat each photo as its own story and piece.
