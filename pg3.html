Skies

This website is dedicated to showcasing my favourite hobby: travel photography

The photos are taken by me, Tekla Mikadze.This page touches on the subject of skies

Another subject that my friends have noticed that I'm really attracted to shooting is clouds. At this point it's an inside joke that whenever I'm outside during sundown I'm compelled to take a picture of the fading light scattered between the clouds. I love capturing the wide range of colors that are sometimes visible in a sunrise or a sundown. Seeing such vibrant colors amidst intricate cloud patterns brings a sense of calm to my eyes.
