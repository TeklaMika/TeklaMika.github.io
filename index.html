Travel Photography

Info:

This website is dedicated to showcasing my favourite hobby: travel photography. The photos are taken by me, Tekla Mikadze

My interest in Travel Photography was sparked once i saw WPA postcards that advertised American national parks. I love their vibrancy, look, vintage colors, and the comfortable feeling they evoke. I also aim to evoke such feelings with my photography. I used to own a professional camera but I noticed most of the photos that I was taking and that I liked the most were the ones that I took impromptu and hadn't thought to bring my camera. So i fully embraced the mobility and practicality of my smartphone and now shoot photos almost exclusively on my phone.
